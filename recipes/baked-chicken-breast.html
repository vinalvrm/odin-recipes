Baked Chicken Breast

Description

Chicken breast is a lean cut of meat from a chicken that is high in protein and is used in various dishes, or eaten by itself. It's perfect for gym bros!

Ingredients

  • 4 ounce skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon coarse sea salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch Creole seasoning, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon chicken broth, or more to taste
